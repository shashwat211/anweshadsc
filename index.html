THE HACK O'LATTE

Hack O' Latte provides a platform for developers to solve real-world problems efficiently and effectively.It is a gathering where programmers code day and night for few days to come up with new and innovative solutions.

The goal of Hack O'Latte woukd be to create a user friendly software at end if event.There would be really cutting-edge problem statements mostly related to our current industrial needs for the competitors to put their thinking caps on!. This Hackthon will also allow participants to come up with their own problem statements.Winners would be aprreciated with exciting prizes and goodies.

To be organised in first week of February 2020,at IIT PATNA,under DEVELOPERS STUDENTS CLUB(DSC) of IIT PATNA in association with Anwesha,this is the very first edition of HACK O'LATTE.We are also planning to conduct some guest sessions.

Reach IIT Patna

IIT Patna is located in a town named Bihta in the outskirts of Patna city which is well connected through railways/airways. You can reach IIT Patna through a regular service of buses/trains from Patna which run every half an hour. Look out for buses with codes 888 or 888A at Gandhi Maidan, Patna which will drop you directly at the college. You can also use the local trains which will run from Patna Junction and will drop you at Bihta Station, from where IIT Patna is a short rickshaw ride. During Anwesha, there will be special buses running from Patna to the college the schedule of which can be found on Anwesha's website/facebook page.
